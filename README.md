## ⚙️ Virtual Fabrication Modules Covered
As part of the simulation coursework (see `Assignments/` folder), the following process modules were built, analyzed, and characterized:

**Oxide Isolation (LOCOS & STI)**
- Studied the LOCOS process and the bird's beak effect
- Analyzed achieved vs. planned isolation trench pitch and its effect on active area
- Studied STI process flow, CMP dishing, pad oxide etch selectivity, and filler oxide overhang control
- Compared isolation approach used in a 14 nm FinFET process

**Metal-First / Metal-Last Gate Process**
- Compared Gate-First and Gate-Last process flows
- Studied Al metal diffusion into the active region during high-temperature anneal
- Extracted maximum anneal time and minimum gate oxide thickness to keep contamination below the device failure limit
- Identified additional process complexity and materials introduced in the gate-last flow

**Spacer Formation**
- Studied conformal deposition techniques used for spacer formation
- Explained lithography-free spacer patterning
- Extracted CD loss (ΔCD) and CD variation along gate height

**Replacement Gate Process & Metrology**
- Studied gate-last process flow and dummy anneal representation of diffusion
- Extracted Al concentration in the active region using Aluminium Profile Export analysis
- Used virtual metrology to correlate Gate CD with etch parameters
- Studied effects of lithography bias, overlay error, and void formation on device functionality

**Ion Implantation & Silicidation**
- Determined maximum activation anneal temperature and allowable tilt angle for proper Fin doping
- Studied the shadowing effect and asymmetrical dopant distribution across Fin width
- Studied Salicidation and Polycide formation, and silicide metal/anneal temperature options

**Damascene & Double Damascene Interconnects**
- Identified key limitations of conventional metallization (etch difficulty, Cu diffusion, oxidation)
- Studied copper diffusion profiles at varying temperatures (550 °C, 750 °C, 1050 °C)
- Evaluated barrier layer materials (Si₃N₄, TiN, W) for diffusion suppression
- Compared single and double damascene process flows, including trench-first vs. via-first sequencing
- Identified etch stop layers, barrier layers, and interconnect metal choices in a 14 nm FinFET reference process
